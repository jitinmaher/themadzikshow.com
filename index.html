Mentalist, Magician, Speaker

MAGIC, MYSTERY AND LAUGHTER

Chandrankit is a renowned magician and mentalist who believes in creating magic. Through his decade long journey, he has fallen in love with the way it inspires people giving them a whole new perspective. As an artist, his goal is to motivate the audience to view things from a wider angle which will open them to an array of ideas and solutions.

Started performing at the age of 7 years and now with more than 10 years of experience as a magician and speaker, Chandrankit with his charismatic and humorous personality has performed thousands of shows, travelling across India entertaining thousands of people in his Magic special "The Madzik Show"
